The melodies of the flute make the friends sway,
With Shyam, the eyes set out to play.
In the streets of Barsana, joy fills the air,
To play Holi with Shyam, Deeya and Vaishnavi is there!

Sometimes with gulal, sometimes with hues,
Kanha hides, enchanting the crews.
With the Gopis, even Deeya and vaishnavi smiled bright,
Once again, she came to bathe in colors' delight!
